The Apex Process

Forge your peak physical state through our structured, metric-driven athlete development workflow.

Step-by-Step

Your 5-Step Roadmap

We do not believe in random efforts. We walk you through a calibrated process that guarantees progress.

01

Biometric Assessment

We begin by mapping your starting coordinate. Your coach compiles body composition analyses (muscle mass vs fat percentages), checks dynamic posture alignments, and tests cardiorespiratory thresholds.

  • Body Mass scan
  • VO2 Max
02

Bespoke Programming

We write your roadmap. We translate your assessment metrics into a custom lifting split. We define weekly hypertrophy loads, sets, progression patterns, and cardio intervals based on your schedules.

  • Custom splits
  • Rep protocols
03

Active Coaching Sessions

Perform with intent. Work 1-on-1 with master coaches to correct barbell mechanical execution, control load speed tempos, and push through mental capacity boundaries safely on the turf.

  • Form correction
  • Velocity cues
04

Macrometric & Fuel Tuning

Refuel intelligently. We supply simple macrometric dietary structures outlining optimal protein feeds and carb ratios. Order custom workout recovery shakes directly via your member portal.

  • Meal breakdowns
  • Refuel shakes
05

Milestone Calibration

Analyze progress and lock in achievements. Every 30 days we run re-assessment scans, measure calorie charts, and update target weights in your dashboard portal to keep you moving forward.

  • Re-assess scan
  • Dashboard updates
